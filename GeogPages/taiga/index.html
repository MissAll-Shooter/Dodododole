TIGA

the cold region

Tiga is made up of 3 regions - Tiga Central, Tiga North and Tiga South.
Tiga South is made up of 7 countries - hilian, kijlin, ukremok, piklikl, grindink, uimoth and acrecate.
Tiga Central is 5 countries - golkok, hancocl, santema, hadze and, the capital country of Tiga, Maintalinkk.
Tiga North is 6 countries - norkl, falykk, fishhl, bridkk, hmuunl and relipitl.
So, that's 18 countries in total.

The main language is Maintalinkkish for all the countries apart from hilian, kijlin and ukremok, who use South Medditan, and hmuunl along with relipitl, speak south Savvnese.
